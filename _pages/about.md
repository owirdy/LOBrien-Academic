I am a PhD candidate in the **Computational Economics Lab** at the **University of Oxford**, where I am pursuing a D.Phil. in Economics (2022–Present). My research focuses on **computational economics and market design**, with particular interests in Auction Theory and Mechanism Design, Agent-Based Macroeconomic Models, and Algorithmic Game Theory.

Prior to Oxford, I earned my B.A. from **Trinity College Dublin** (2018–2022), where I was awarded a **Foundation Scholarship**.

## Research Interests

- **Auction Theory and Mechanism Design**
- **Agent-Based Macroeconomic Models**
- **Algorithmic Game Theory**
